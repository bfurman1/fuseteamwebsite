Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Boris Furman ("I," "me," or "my"), a sole proprietor based in the United States, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect information when you interact with my time tracking service, including the SMS text messaging program.

I provide a time tracking application designed for Landman organizations and the independent landmen, field agents, and contractors who work with them. The individuals who submit time and receive SMS messages through the platform ("End Users") provide their mobile phone numbers directly to me when enrolling in the service.

1. Information We Collect

  • End User information: Name, mobile phone number, email address, and work identifier (such as a crew or project assignment). End Users provide this information directly to me when they enroll in the SMS service.
  • Time tracking data: Hours worked, dates, project or well codes, location or tract references, and related timekeeping information submitted by End Users via SMS or web interface.
  • Message logs: Records of SMS messages sent and received through the service, retained for operational, support, and compliance purposes.
  • Technical information: Log data, IP addresses, device identifiers, and usage metrics related to the website and platform.

2. How I Use Information

I use collected information to:

  • Deliver time tracking reminders and late-submission notices to End Users.
  • Receive, process, and store time tracking data submitted via SMS or web.
  • Provide the service and respond to support requests (HELP keyword, email, web).
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the platform.
  • Comply with legal, tax, and contractual obligations.

3. SMS Messaging Program

No sharing of mobile information for marketing. Mobile phone numbers and SMS opt-in consent will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information is shared only with subcontractors that support the operation of the messaging service (such as Twilio) and mobile carriers required to deliver messages.

End Users enroll in the SMS program by providing their mobile phone number directly (through the web enrollment form or by texting the keyword START to the designated Twilio number) and confirming they consent to receive time tracking messages. Upon enrollment, End Users receive a confirmation message describing the program and how to opt out.

Message frequency varies based on timesheet deadlines (typically 1–5 messages per week). Message and data rates may apply. End Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP, which immediately removes them from all SMS communications. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact me at the address below.

4. Information Sharing

I do not sell personal information. I share information only with:

  • Service providers that help operate the platform (including Twilio for SMS delivery, and hosting and analytics providers), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • The Landman organization that engages the End User, for the purpose of timekeeping, payroll, project billing, and time tracking administration.
  • Legal authorities when required by law, subpoena, or court order.

5. Data Security

I use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls, and audit logging. No system is perfectly secure, but I take these obligations seriously and will notify affected users of any material security incident affecting their data as required by law.

6. Data Retention

I retain End User information for as long as the service is actively used, and for a reasonable period thereafter to meet legal and tax recordkeeping obligations. End Users may request deletion of information by contacting me, subject to any overriding legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your state or country of residence, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict the processing of your personal information, and to opt out of certain disclosures. U.S. residents in states such as California, Colorado, Virginia, Connecticut, and others with applicable privacy laws may exercise these rights by contacting me at the address below. I will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to individuals under 18, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be communicated to active users.
