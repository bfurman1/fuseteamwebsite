Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  HaloTime / Boris Furman

This Privacy Policy describes how Boris Furman ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the HaloTime platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, employer/company name.
  • Work data: time entries, expense records, absence records submitted through the platform.
  • Mobile phone number: collected only if you opt in to SMS notifications.
  • SMS consent records: date, time, and nature of your SMS opt-in or opt-out choice.
  • Usage data: IP address, browser type, and pages visited for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the HaloTime time tracking and billing platform.
  • To send SMS notifications if you have opted in (time reminders, approval notices, submission confirmations).
  • To generate invoices and reports for client companies.
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud.

3. SMS & Mobile Data

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Your mobile number is used solely to deliver the SMS notifications you requested. It is shared only with Twilio (our SMS delivery provider) and mobile carriers as required to deliver messages. Message frequency: 1–5 messages per week. Msg & data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. See our SMS Program Terms for full details.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Your employer / client company — time and expense data you submit is visible to your approvers and administrators.
  • Twilio — SMS delivery provider (phone number only, for message delivery).
  • Microsoft Azure — cloud infrastructure hosting the platform.
  • Google — authentication provider (OAuth sign-in only; no data is shared beyond authentication).

5. Data Security

HaloTime is hosted on Microsoft Azure with encrypted connections (TLS), private database endpoints, and row-level security enforced at the database layer. We do not store passwords.

6. Data Retention

Account and work data is retained for as long as your account is active or as required by your employer's contractual obligations. SMS consent records are retained for compliance audit purposes.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the address below. SMS opt-out requests are honored immediately upon receipt of a STOP message.
