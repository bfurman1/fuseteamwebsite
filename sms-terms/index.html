1. How to Opt In

You enroll by providing your mobile phone number and checking the consent box at halotime-int.fuseteam.ai/sms-opt-in/. Enrollment is voluntary and not required to use HaloTime.

After enrolling you will receive a confirmation message:

HaloTime: You are now subscribed to HaloTime Time Entry Application. Msg&data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies (1–5/wk). Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel. Terms: halotime-int.fuseteam.ai/sms-terms/

2. How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive a final confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

HaloTime: You have been unsubscribed. No more messages will be sent. Reply START to re-subscribe. Privacy: halotime-int.fuseteam.ai/privacy-policy/
