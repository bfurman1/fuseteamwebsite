SMS Program Terms

Last updated: April 22, 2026

These terms apply specifically to the SMS text messaging program operated by Boris Furman, a sole proprietor based in the United States ("I," "me," or "my"). By enrolling in the program or texting START to the designated Twilio number, you agree to these SMS Program Terms, in addition to the Privacy Policy.

Program Name: Landman Time Tracking Reminders

Purpose: Time tracking reminders, late-submission notices, and time entry submission via SMS chat for landmen, field agents, and contractors using the service.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on timesheet deadlines, typically 1–5 messages per week.

Message and Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply per your mobile carrier plan.

1. How to Opt In

You enroll in this program by providing your mobile phone number directly — either through the web enrollment form or by texting the keyword START to the designated Twilio number — and confirming you consent to receive time tracking messages. Enrollment is voluntary.

After enrolling, you will receive a confirmation message similar to:

Time Tracking: You are now subscribed to time tracking reminders. Msg&data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies (1-5/wk). Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel.

2. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of the program at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a final confirmation message, after which no further messages will be sent. Example:

Time Tracking: You have been unsubscribed from time tracking reminders. No more messages will be sent. Reply START to re-subscribe.

3. Getting Help

Reply HELP to any message to receive support information. Example:

Time Tracking reminders. For support, email boris_furman@fuseteam.ai. Reply STOP to cancel.

4. Sample Messages You May Receive

Time Tracking: Your hours for week ending 04/19 have not been submitted. Reply with your hours to log them. Reply STOP to opt out.
Time Tracking: Reminder — time entries are due by 5pm Friday. Reply with your hours or HELP for help. STOP to cancel.
Time Tracking: Entry received. Total: 40 hours logged for week of 04/13. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel.

Mobile information is shared only with service providers that support the operation of the messaging program (such as Twilio) and mobile carriers required to deliver the messages. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
